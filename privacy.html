Privacy Policy

Last updated:

FinalSay.ai (“we”, “us”, or “our”) respects your privacy. This Policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

Information We Collect

  • Input Content: Text you paste to generate replies. We process this to produce the output. We don’t sell your content.
  • Technical Data: Basic analytics (IP, device info, usage events) to improve reliability and prevent abuse.
  • Optional Contact: If you join our waitlist or contact us, we store your email to send updates and support.

How We Use Information

  • Generate AI responses you request.
  • Operate, secure, and improve FinalSay.ai.
  • Communicate with you about changes, issues, or new features.

Data Retention

Generated requests may be cached briefly for performance and abuse prevention. We don’t keep permanent logs of your pasted content beyond what’s required for security, debugging, or legal compliance.

Third‑Party Services

We use reputable providers (e.g., hosting, analytics, AI APIs like OpenAI). These processors handle data under their own privacy terms.

Security

We use industry‑standard measures to protect data. No method is 100% secure; please use the service responsibly.

Your Choices

  • You can choose not to provide optional info.
  • You can request deletion of contact data by emailing support@finalsay.ai.

Children

FinalSay.ai is not intended for children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy. We’ll post the new version here with an updated date.
